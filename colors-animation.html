Colors & Animations

Design | Colors | Animations

A mockup landing page with an applied color palette, dark theme and animations. The primary focus was to create a color palette and validate its contrast, and to then apply it to the design.

SEM3 | Assignment | 3 days

Process

I began by creating a basic color palette with huemint.com, and then expanded on this palette with some greyscale colors and lighter versions of my accent colors. I validated the contrasts with Adobe's contrast analyzer, and then began applying my palette to the design.

Once the initial colors were applied, I added small animations to buttons and links, and then began working on a dark theme for the design. I based the dark theme on the same color palette, but adjusted certain colors to better contrast with dark backgrounds. I then implemented a switch in the navigation menu to toggle the dark theme.
