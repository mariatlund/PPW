Design with Frameworks

Design | Frameworks | Pico

Building a mockup landing page using a design framework. I made use of Pico.css, but we were also introduced to Bootstrap and Tailwind. The main purpose of this was to play around with design frameworks and discover the benefits as well as the disadvantages of them.

SEM3 | Assignment | 2 days

Process

I first created a small landingpage with Bootstrap, but wasn't quite happy with it. I tried out Pico after that, and found that it was more simple and very consistent in how it styled elements. So, I continued with Pico and applied basic container and grid styling to the different elements to create a responsive layout.

Once I had a functional layout, I did some small adjustments to the styling to add colors and some simple hover effects. I also added stock images to play around with different image dimensions in the design.
