About

The Basics

I'm a 23 year old digital designer based in Copenhagen, currently studying Multimedia Design at KEA and working as a Student Assistant for a fintech startup. I grew up abroad and am still an expat at heart.

I have a love for all things creative and have worked with both traditional and digital artforms all my life, my digital art journey starting with horrible little frame-by-frame animations on a Nintendo DS.

Experience

While studying full time, I also work part time as a Student Assistant at Linconomy, a fintech startup. Here I work with various design and UX tasks, developing a brand new website and web platform.

In the past, I've worked for Sunweb and Eliza was here as a Content Marketer, both on a full time and freelance basis. During this time, I managed and created content for websites, newsletters and SoMe. I worked with systems such as Sitecore, and also performed SEO optimisations.
